# 🚀 Revenue & Profit Analysis Dashboard

## 📌 Project Overview
This project analyzes **revenue trends, regional performance, and product profitability** in the beverage industry.

The main objective is to transform raw data into **actionable business insights** that can support better decision-making.

---

## 🎯 Objectives
- Analyze revenue trends over time  
- Identify top-performing and underperforming regions  
- Evaluate product-level performance  
- Provide data-driven business recommendations  

---

## 📊 Key Insights

### 📈 Revenue Trends
- Revenue fluctuates significantly throughout the year  
- Peak performance occurs in **mid-year and year-end periods**  
- Indicates possible **seasonality or promotional impact**

### 🌍 Regional Performance
- **West region** generates the highest revenue (~2.4M)  
- **Midwest region** has the lowest contribution  
- Suggests demand concentration in specific regions  

### 🥤 Product Performance
- **Coca-Cola** is the top-performing product (~1.91M revenue)  
- Acts as the **main driver of overall sales and profitability**  
- Lower-performing products contribute less significantly  

### 💰 Profitability
- Average profit per unit: **0.39**  
- West region is the most profitable  
- Profit margins are relatively consistent across products  

---

## 💡 Business Recommendations
- Focus on **high-performing regions** to maximize profit  
- Improve **low-performing regions** with targeted strategies  
- Leverage **top-selling products (Coca-Cola)**  
- Optimize **mid-performing products** for balanced revenue
